Barack Obama

It has always been the bold, the most active, the builders of things (some recognized but mostly men and women whose efforts remain in obscurity) who have propelled us on the long and arduous path to prosperity and freedom.

President Obama's years of service reflect an unwavering belief that it is possible to unite people around a purposeful policy. In the Illinois Senate, President Obama promoted the most significant ethics reform in twenty-five years, cut taxes for families in the workforce, and expanded health care for children and their parents. As an Illinois state senator, he worked with the opposition to moderate the influence of lobbyists, secure weapons of mass destruction, and promote government transparency by reporting all government spending online.
